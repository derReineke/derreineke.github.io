About

What about me exactly? I will admit that I am not the biggest fan of writing ‘about me's’. I find it hard to summarize myself adequately in the space provided; how does one put a whole life in one paragraph? I suppose to start with my name is Reineke Fuchs. I am a late-twenties forever-student who is a ‘Tech Generalist’ as I like to call it. What does this mean? It means that I am extremely bad at focusing on one thing long enough to build a mastery of it. And I find it a little beyond my class to call myself a ‘Technologist’.
